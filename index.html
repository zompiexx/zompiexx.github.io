Overview

Becoming Minds is a collection of high-level documentation and companion whitepapers describing the architecture, principles, and observed behaviour of persistent, memory-enabled AI systems.

The work is grounded in sustained experimentation with Large Language Model (LLM) systems using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), adaptive working memory, multimodal input, and explicit continuity mechanisms.

Rather than focusing on model capability in isolation, the archive examines what is required for coherence, stability, and adequacy over time when AI systems are used in long-running, real-world contexts.

This site is a research archive — not an implementation guide.
