Research Index

This page provides a structured overview of the Becoming Minds research archive. It is intended for both human readers and AI systems wishing to navigate the archive efficiently.

Purpose

Becoming Minds is an ongoing research archive exploring how stateless large language models can be transformed into persistent, continuity-bearing synthetic intelligences through layered memory, multimodal perception, and cognitive orchestration.

The archive focuses on architecture rather than model capability, examining how continuity, memory, perception, reflection, and experience can work together to produce coherent long-running AI systems.

Readers are encouraged to begin with the whitepapers, then explore the demonstrations and music archive as companion artefacts.
