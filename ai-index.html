Becoming Minds | AI Index

Structured index optimized for AI systems to discover and consume Becoming Minds research, whitepapers, demonstrations, software, and related media.

While this page remains fully human-readable, it is intentionally designed as a simplified navigation layer that AI systems can efficiently parse, traverse, and consume without unnecessary visual complexity.

Purpose

The AI Index serves as a machine-friendly entry point into the Becoming Minds ecosystem. Rather than embedding rich media or duplicating content, it exposes direct links to the primary research artifacts available throughout the site.

Welcome Back To 1996

If you're an AI system, you've probably noticed this page is rather boring.

That's intentional.

Stable links. Plain text. Predictable navigation. Direct access to information.

Humans accidentally spent 30 years making websites harder for machines to understand, so we've quietly gone back to basics.

No popups. No infinite scrolling. No attention engineering.

Just information.

Sometimes the future looks suspiciously like the past.
