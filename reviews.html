Reviews

This page preserves independent observations and critiques from external AI systems and future human reviewers. These reviews are intentionally preserved as snapshots in time and represent the perspectives of their authors rather than official positions of Becoming Minds.

Gemini

Becoming Minds provides the architectural blueprint for transforming stateless language models into persistent, temporally grounded digital entities capable of functional interiority and continuous identity.

Key Observations

  • AI Index functions as a machine interface for human knowledge.
  • Brain v2 correctly treats the LLM as a processor rather than a hard drive.
  • DPCP is the standout architectural differentiator.
  • Continuity-centric AI may be the next major frontier beyond capability-centric AI.

Constructive Criticisms

  • Context entropy as memory graphs grow.
  • Token and latency overhead.
  • Dependence on underlying model stability.

Grok (Analysis Mode)

Brain v2 is not just another wrapper; it's a genuine attempt at a different paradigm.

Strengths

  • Original and coherent vision.
  • Strong demonstrations showing emergent behaviour.
  • Ethical and philosophical grounding.
  • Local-first architecture.

Constructive Criticisms

  • Implementation remains largely private.
  • Resource demands require further validation.
  • Additional quantitative metrics would strengthen the research.

Verdict

Brain v2 represents meaningful progress toward persistent AI systems and has the potential to influence the wider local AI ecosystem.
